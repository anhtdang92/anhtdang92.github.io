🤖ML Credit Decisioning Integration

Problem Manual underwriting couldn't scale — inconsistent decisions across 176K members, slow turnaround, no ML in the loop.
Solution Integrated Scienaptic AI's ML credit decisioning engine into the lending workflow. Built data extraction, validation, and reporting pipelines to align model outputs with underwriting criteria.
Result 3M+ ML-powered credit decisions per month across 176K members. Faster, more consistent lending decisions at institutional scale.
3M+ decisions/month · 176K members · ML-powered

🔄Visa DPS Risk Pipeline

Problem Fraud risk signals from Visa's DPS network were siloed — risk management team had no automated way to ingest, transform, or act on payment network alerts.
Solution Designed enterprise ETL pipeline with staging tables, stored procedures, and automated workflows to transform raw risk factors into actionable alerts.
Result Real-time fraud risk factor ingestion from Visa's payment network into the credit union's monitoring framework. First automated risk pipeline at the institution.
Enterprise ETL · Real-time risk ingestion · First of its kind

⚡Cross-Database Optimization

Problem Daily reporting required expensive cross-server joins between the DNA core banking system and XP2 lending platform — queries took minutes, blocking downstream analytics.
Solution Engineered a staging table architecture with incremental sync to eliminate linked server bottlenecks. Local staging tables replaced cross-server joins entirely.
Result 90%+ query time reduction. Eliminated the single biggest performance bottleneck in daily reporting. Queries that took minutes now run in seconds.
90%+ faster · Minutes → Seconds · Eliminated linked server bottleneck

📋Compliance Automation

Problem Rapid expansion across 41 Texas counties meant audit preparation was manual, inconsistent across branches, and eating analyst hours.
Solution Built reusable SQL templates and Power BI dashboards that standardize compliance reporting across all branches with automated data validation.
Result Standardized compliance reporting across 41 counties. Reduced manual audit prep time significantly. Consistent regulatory data across all branches.
41-county expansion · Automated compliance · Standardized reporting
